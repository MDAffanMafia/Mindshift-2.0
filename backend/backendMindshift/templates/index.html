What is Bipolar Disorder?

Bipolar disorder is a mental health condition characterized by extreme mood swings that include manic episodes (highs) and depressive episodes (lows).

Self-Assessment

If you suspect you may have bipolar disorder, you can use self-assessment tools to better understand your mood and behavior. However, these should not replace a professional evaluation.
